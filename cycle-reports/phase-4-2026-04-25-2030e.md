## Gap fixed
/contact's LocalBusiness JSON-LD was missing three Schema.org-recommended
fields that Google uses to enrich local-pack listings:

- `priceRange` ("$$") — drives the price-tier indicator on the
  knowledge panel.
- `paymentAccepted` ("Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card") — drives the
  payment-icon row in the local pack.
- `currenciesAccepted` ("USD") — explicit so international clients
  searching "Inspire Courts pickleball Arizona" don't see a currency
  ambiguity flag.

Added all three. The /contact LocalBusiness block is now feature-complete
against Google's local-business structured-data spec.
