Phase 7 wrapped up rotation 1. The duplication and admin-UX gaps were tackled together since admin-UX issues kept surfacing during the code-quality audit:

- Extracted `requireCronSecret()` into `src/lib/api-auth.ts` and applied it to all 7 cron routes (`billing-tick`, `churn-tick`, `inquiry-sla-tick`, `journey-tick`, `digest`, `maintenance`, `member-daily`). ~110 lines removed from cron handlers.

- `any` audit confirmed clean across new lib files.

### Admin UX (P0/P1 issues found mid-iteration)

- Added reset-filter shortcut, click-to-sort columns, sticky table headers, shimmer skeleton loaders in place of plain "Loading…" text.
- Surfaced load errors with explicit retry button instead of silent failure.
- Friendly not-found states on `/members/[id]` and `/rentals/[id]`.
- A11y: `aria-label` on filter inputs + `aria-live` for result counts.

### Performance
- Folded `members.lastVisitAt` into a correlated subquery (single round-trip vs. N+1).
