## Current state
Audit of the homepage SportsActivityLocation JSON-LD turned up two real gaps:

1. **Pickleball missing from `sport[]`.** The site has a /pickleball microsite
   and a SportMicrosite SportsActivity LD block for it, but the canonical
   homepage LD only lists Basketball, Volleyball, Futsal, Jiu-Jitsu. Local SEO
   "what's offered here" cards key off this list — pickleball was invisible.
2. **No `openingHoursSpecification`.** /contact's LocalBusiness has full daily
   hours, but the homepage SportsActivityLocation (the `@id: SITE_URL` canonical
   block) doesn't. Google's knowledge panel has to infer hours from /contact
   when the homepage is the natural place to declare them.
