## Gaps found this iteration

### P0 — fix this iteration
1. **`/api/scores/live/[gameId]` had no rate limit** — spectator pages poll every 3s. CDN at `Cache-Control: max-age=2` absorbs most traffic, but a single bad actor can still hammer the origin. Capped at 60 req/min/IP.
2. **`/api/tournaments/schedule` had no rate limit** — calls Google Sheets each origin hit. CDN-cached at `s-maxage=300`, but Google quotas are real and a noisy script could burn them. Capped at 30 req/min/IP.
